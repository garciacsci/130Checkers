How to Play

Welcome to the CSCI 130: Checkers Game! Here are the basic instructions on how to play:

  1. Checkers is a two-player game played on an 8x8 or 10x10 board.
  2. Players take turns moving their pieces diagonally forward.
  3. Capture your opponent's pieces by jumping over them.
  4. If you can capture, then capturing is your only move.
  5. The game ends when one player captures all of the opponent's pieces or blocks them from moving.

Controls

Use the following controls to play the game:

  • Select Piece: Click on your piece to select it.
  • Move Piece: Click on a valid square to move your selected piece.
